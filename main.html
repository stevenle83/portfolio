Hi my name is Steven Le. I am working out of New Orleans, Louisiana. I was a small business owner from 2007-2014. After selling my business I've decided to pursue a career in Web Development. I started off coding at codeacademy and realized that I really enjoyed building something from scratch and seeing it all happen on screen. Codeacademy was a good introduction to coding, but I was seeking more challenging projects. I did my research and stumbled upon Thinkful.com. I enrolled in Thinkful's front-end development course and here I am, buildinng a number of projects and creating my own portfolio website. I've learned how to build projects from scratch using my knowledge of HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, AJAX/JSON, and the Twitter Bootstrap 3 framework. We were often given assignments where we'd have to used existing projects with HTML and CSS through Github, and add our own javascript codes to complete the assignment. I've learned how to work with others on projects, as well as working independently. I look forward to putting my aquired skills to use and pursue my passion for coding.
